Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Collect

LipLearn is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Camera Data: Video feed is processed locally on your device for lip reading analysis. No video data is stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Learning Progress: Your quiz scores, completed lessons, and achievements are stored locally in your browser.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service (optional).

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide real-time lip reading analysis
  • Track your learning progress and achievements
  • Improve our AI models and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All camera processing happens locally on your device
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No permanent storage of biometric data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Export your learning progress
