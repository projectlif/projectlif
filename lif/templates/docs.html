Getting Started

Welcome sa Project LIF! Ang gabay na ito ay tutulong sa iyo sa pagsisimula gamitin ang aming AI-powered lip reading platform.

Quick Start

  1. Pumunta sa Subukan gamit ang AI page
  2. Bigyan ng access ang camera.
  3. Click "Start Camera" para simulan ang real-time analysis
  4. Magsalita ng natural at tingnan ang magiging predictions.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Webcam or built-in camera
  • Maliwanag at malinaw na environment
  • Stable na internet connection

Camera Setup

Optimal Setup

  • Position: Camera at eye level
  • Distance: 2-3 feet from camera
  • Lighting: Face well-lit, avoid backlighting
  • Background: Plain, non-distracting

Troubleshooting Camera Issues

  • Tingnan kung gumagana ba ang camera.
  • Siguraduhin na ang browser ay may camera permissions
  • I-try mag-refresh ng page
  • Tingnan kung may ibang application na gumagamit ng camera

Quiz System

How Quizzes Work

Our quiz system tests your lip reading skills through visual recognition challenges:

  • Watch GIF demonstrations of syllable pronunciations
  • Choose the correct syllable from multiple options
  • Earn points based on accuracy and speed
  • Build streaks for bonus points

Scoring System

Action Base Points Bonus Conditions
Correct Answer 10 points +5 for speed, +5 for streak
Streak Bonus - +5 at 3 streak, +10 at 5 streak
Difficulty Bonus - +3 medium, +5 hard

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Low prediction accuracy
  • Improve lighting conditions
  • Position face directly toward camera
  • Speak more clearly and deliberately
  • Remove obstructions from mouth area
Camera feed not working
  • Check browser permissions
  • Ensure camera is not in use by other apps
  • Try a different browser
  • Restart your device
Slow performance
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Check internet connection
  • Update your browser
  • Clear browser cache
