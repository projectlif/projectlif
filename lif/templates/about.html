Our Technology

AI-Powered Recognition

Our proprietary deep learning models, built on 3D CNNs + LSTM and Bi LSTM with temporal analysis, are trained on curated lip-reading datasets. By standardizing lighting and applying preprocessing enhancements such as CLAHE and sharpening, we achieve reliable predictions across different speakers under controlled recording conditions.

Real-Time Processing

Advanced computer vision algorithms process video streams in real-time, providing instant feedback without compromising user privacy.

Privacy-First Design

All processing happens locally on your device. No video data is transmitted or stored on our servers, ensuring complete privacy protection.

Adaptive Learning

Our platform adapts to individual learning styles and progress, providing personalized recommendations and difficulty adjustments.
